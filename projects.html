Project 2 - Intern DTR System

The Intern DTR System is a web-based application designed to track and manage the attendance of interns in a company. The system provides an efficient and user-friendly way for interns to record their daily attendance, either by scanning a QR code (for employees within the office) or manually entering their attendance using their smartphones (for remote work or when they are outside the office). The system also includes an Admin Side where company administrators can easily monitor intern activity, edit their profiles, and ensure the smooth functioning of the attendance process.
